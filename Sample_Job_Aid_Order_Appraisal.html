Appraisal Ordering Guide

Standard operating procedure for ordering residential appraisals via the Apex Appraisal Management portal.

1 Pre-Order Checklist

Ensure you have the following ready before starting the order:

  • ✓ Executed Sales Contract (for Purchases)
  • ✓ Subject Property Address & Legal Description
  • ✓ Borrower Contact Information (Phone & Email)
  • ✓ Intent to Proceed (Signed by Borrower)

2 Order Process

Log in to Apex AMC

Navigate to portal.apexappraisal.demo and enter your internal credentials.

Note: If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Do not create a new account.

New Order Entry

Select "New Order" from the dashboard. Choose the correct loan program (Conventional, FHA, or VA).

Property & Contact Details

Enter the property address. Ensure the contact for access (Owner, Tenant, or Agent) is clearly identified with a valid phone number.

Pro Tip: Providing the Agent's cell phone number significantly speeds up inspection scheduling.

Review & Payment

Confirm the fee disclosure matches the LE. Select "Bill to Lender" unless specific instructions specify "Pay at Door".
